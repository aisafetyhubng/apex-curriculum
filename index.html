About This Curriculum

Built for clarity. Designed for people who are serious about doing this work.

The APEx curriculum does not assume you already know what AI safety is or which part of it you belong in. It is designed to take you from a motivated but uncertain starting point to a concrete, honest personal pathway plan over four structured weeks. Each week builds on the last. Each session has assigned readings, reflection exercises, and facilitated discussion.

The resources you will read and engage with come from 80,000 Hours, BlueDot Impact, LessWrong, the EA Forum, the AI Alignment Forum, Successif, MATS, Apart Research, and primary academic and policy sources. The framing is always specific to Nigeria, to Africa, and to what it actually means to contribute to this field from here.

Week 0 is optional and is designed for participants who have little or no prior knowledge of AI and want to build a foundation before the programme begins. If you already understand AI concepts and terminology, you can skip Week 0 and join directly from Week 1.

How to Use This Curriculum

Three things to know before you begin.

01
Read before the session
Each week has required pre-session readings. These are non-negotiable. The facilitated discussion assumes you have done the reading. Completing recommended readings is encouraged for participants considering a specific pathway.
02
Engage with the exercises
Reflection exercises and skill audits are not homework. They are how you build your Personal Pathway Plan, which is the main deliverable you leave the programme with. They are worth taking seriously.
03
Access is managed per cohort
Each week's document is linked below. Access is granted to enrolled participants. If a link prompts you to request access, apply to the current APEx cohort through the main programme page.
